Mobile Door Detection: A Computer Vision Tutorial

Supporting Indoor Wayfinding for Seniors and Low-Vision Users

By: Nandini

Welcome to This Tutorial

This interactive tutorial explores the fascinating world of door detection on smartphones, a critical technology for supporting indoor navigation for seniors and people with low vision. Doors serve as essential landmarks in indoor environments, and detecting them accurately can significantly improve accessibility and independence for users with mobility or vision challenges.
